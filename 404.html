About BIRD

Practical support for research teams

BIRD is a Montréal-based bioinformatics consulting service supporting academic laboratories, research teams, students, biotech startups, and life sciences organizations. We help researchers make complex biological data analysis clearer, reproducible, well documented, and easier to transfer to students, trainees, and collaborators.

Services

Core service areas

Flexible project-based or ongoing support adapted to your lab’s needs, budget, and timeline.

Study Design & Bioinformatics Consultation

Planning support before data generation or analysis.

  • Research question and analysis strategy
  • Sample groups, controls, and replicates
  • Metadata and sample organization
  • Sequencing and downstream analysis planning
  • Data management and reproducibility advice

Omics & NGS Data Analysis

Support for omics and sequencing data analysis.

  • RNA-seq and differential expression
  • Genomics and variant-analysis support
  • PhIP-seq and peptide-enrichment analysis
  • Quality control, exploratory analysis, figures, and reports
  • Reproducible scripts and documentation

PhIP-seq & Peptide-Library Design

Specialized support for PhIP-seq and peptide-library design.

  • Peptide tiling strategy
  • Antigen, protein, organism, or target selection
  • FASTA and protein sequence preparation
  • Annotation tables and control peptide planning
  • Downstream enrichment-analysis strategy

Pipelines, Automation & Troubleshooting

Support with computational workflows and technical bottlenecks.

  • Python, R, Bash, and Linux troubleshooting
  • Pipeline development and optimization
  • FASTQ organization and data-transfer workflows
  • HPC and SLURM/Torque support on Alliance clusters
  • README files, SOPs, and workflow handover

Training for Biologists & Research Teams

Personalized training for students and research team members.

  • Python, R, and Bash for biologists
  • Basics of bioinformatics and sequencing
  • Public databases, including NCBI, SRA, GEO, GO, and BLAST
  • Biological data file formats, including FASTA, FASTQ, BAM, VCF, and GTF/GFF
  • Research data organization and reproducible workflows

Mentorship & Scientific Guidance

Technical guidance for students and research teams.

  • Student project support
  • Analysis planning and code review
  • Data interpretation support
  • Methods and workflow documentation
  • Scientific and technical communication support

Training

Capacity building for biologists and research teams

Training can be offered as one-on-one sessions, group workshops, or project-based mentoring using examples that match your team’s data and goals.

  • Python, R, and Bash for biologists
  • Basic bioinformatics concepts
  • Sequencing technologies and NGS basics
  • FASTA, FASTQ, BAM, VCF, and GTF/GFF files
  • NCBI, SRA, GEO, GO, BLAST, and public databases
  • Linux, HPC, Alliance clusters, and reproducible workflows
