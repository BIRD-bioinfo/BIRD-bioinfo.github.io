About BIRD

Practical support for research teams

BIRD is a Montréal-based bioinformatics consulting service supporting academic and life sciences laboratories, scientists, research teams, and students. We help teams make complex biological, omics, sequencing, and research data analyses clearer, reproducible, well documented, and easier to transfer to students, trainees, and collaborators.

Why BIRD?

Senior expertise for clear, reproducible analysis

BIRD combines technical bioinformatics expertise with practical guidance, hands-on training, and clear documentation so teams can understand, reproduce, and continue their analyses with confidence.

Senior bioinformatics experience

More than 10 years of experience supporting multidisciplinary academic research projects and bioinformatics platforms.

Reproducible workflows

Emphasis on clear project structure, documented methods, transferable scripts, and analysis handover.

Training that builds autonomy

Practical teaching and mentorship for scientists, students, research staff, and interdisciplinary teams.

Responsible data practices

Support for organizing metadata, access-controlled files, sensitive biological data, and responsible research workflows.

Services

How BIRD can support your team

Practical support for research teams working with life science, biological, omics, sequencing, and research data, from planning to analysis, documentation and training.

Study Design & Bioinformatics Consultation

Bioinformatics guidance at the planning stage, including sample and metadata structure, replicate strategy, comparison design and analysis strategy.

Omics & Sequencing Data Analysis

End-to-end support for omics and sequencing projects, including quality control, exploratory analysis, WGS, WES, RNA-seq, scRNA-seq, ChIP-seq, amplicon-seq, small RNA-seq, methyl-seq, ATAC-seq, PhIP-seq, metagenomics, metabarcoding and resistome analysis, with interpretation and preparation of clear figures, tables and reports.

Pipelines, Data Organization & Troubleshooting

Development, optimization and troubleshooting of Python, R and Bash workflows, with support for HPC and job-scheduler environments, including Slurm/Torque, automation and reproducible project structure.

Training for life scientists & research teams

Practical training in Python, R, Bash, bioinformatics and data-analysis fundamentals, sequencing basics, biological databases, file formats, research data organization, HPC and Alliance cluster basics, adapted to the learner’s background and project needs.

Mentorship & Scientific Guidance

Scientific and technical guidance for students, trainees and research teams, including project planning, code review, data interpretation, workflow documentation and knowledge transfer.

Research Data Management

Support for organizing research data, metadata, documentation, access-controlled files and project structure to improve clarity, reproducibility and workflow handover.

Training

Capacity building for scientists and research teams

Training can be offered as one-on-one sessions, group workshops, or project-based mentoring using examples that match your team’s data and goals.

  • Python, R, and Bash for scientists
  • Basic bioinformatics concepts
  • Sequencing technologies and NGS basics
  • FASTA, FASTQ, BAM, VCF, and GTF/GFF files
  • NCBI, SRA, GEO, GO, BLAST, and public databases
  • Linux and Bash for researchers
  • HPC and Alliance cluster basics
  • Research data organization and reproducibility
  • Workflow documentation and handover
  • Practical project-based training
