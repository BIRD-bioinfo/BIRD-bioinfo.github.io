About BIRD

Practical bioinformatics support for research teams

BIRD is a Montréal-based bioinformatics consulting service supporting academic laboratories, life-sciences organizations, research teams, scientists, and students.

We help clients plan and perform biological, omics, and sequencing analyses, develop reproducible workflows, organize research data, and create clear documentation that can be shared and reused.

Support is adapted to each project, from focused technical assistance to complete analysis, training, and ongoing collaboration.

Who we are

Two bioinformaticians, one shared vision

BIRD brings together two bioinformaticians with more than 15 years of combined experience in biological research, genomics, programming, data analysis, scientific training, and knowledge transfer.

Our foundation in biology helps us understand the scientific questions behind each project, while our computational experience allows us to work across diverse genomic and next-generation sequencing technologies, data types, and research environments.

We created BIRD because we believe bioinformatics should be rigorous without being inaccessible. We work collaboratively, explain our decisions clearly, and provide clients with the knowledge and tools needed to understand their analyses and continue their work with greater confidence and independence.

Services

How BIRD can support your team

Practical support for research teams working with life science, biological, omics, sequencing, and research data, from planning to analysis, documentation and training.

Study Design and Bioinformatics Consultation

Bioinformatics guidance at the planning stage, including sample and metadata structure, replicate strategy, comparison design and analysis strategy.

Omics and Sequencing Data Analysis

End-to-end analysis for genomics, transcriptomics, epigenomics, amplicon, metagenomics, and other sequencing projects, including quality control, interpretation, figures, tables, and reporting.

Pipeline Development and Troubleshooting

Development, optimization and troubleshooting of Python, R and Bash workflows, with support for HPC and job-scheduler environments, including Slurm/Torque, automation and reproducible project structure.

Bioinformatics Training

Practical training in Python, R, Bash, bioinformatics and data-analysis fundamentals, sequencing basics, biological databases, file formats, research data organization, HPC and Alliance cluster basics, adapted to the learner’s background and project needs.

Research Mentorship and Scientific Guidance

Scientific and technical guidance for students, trainees and research teams, including project planning, code review, data interpretation, workflow documentation and knowledge transfer.

Research Data Management

Support for organizing research data, metadata, documentation, access-controlled files and project structure to improve clarity, reproducibility and workflow handover.

Training

Practical bioinformatics training for scientists and research teams

Training can be offered as one-on-one sessions, group workshops, or project-based mentoring using examples that match your team’s data and goals.

  • Sequencing technologies and NGS fundamentals

  • HPC, SLURM, and Alliance cluster fundamentals

  • Workflow development, documentation, and handover
  • Practical project-based training
