About BIRD

Practical support for research teams

BIRD is a Montréal-based bioinformatics consulting service supporting academic laboratories, research teams, students, biotech startups, and life sciences organizations. We help researchers make complex biological data analysis clearer, reproducible, well documented, and easier to transfer to students, trainees, and collaborators.

BIRD brings senior bioinformatics experience from academic research environments, including omics and NGS analysis, research data organization, reproducible workflow development, training, mentorship, and support for sensitive biological and genomic datasets.

Why BIRD?

Senior expertise with a human-centered approach

BIRD combines technical bioinformatics expertise with patient, practical training and clear documentation so teams can understand, reproduce, and continue their analyses with confidence.

Senior bioinformatics experience

More than 10 years of experience supporting multidisciplinary academic research projects and bioinformatics platforms.

Reproducible workflows

Emphasis on clear project structure, documented methods, transferable scripts, and analysis handover.

Training that builds autonomy

Practical teaching and mentorship for biologists, students, research staff, and interdisciplinary teams.

Responsible data practices

Support for organizing metadata, access-controlled files, sensitive biological data, and responsible research workflows.

Services

How BIRD can support your team?

A concise service menu for laboratories, research teams, students, and life sciences organizations.

Study Design & Bioinformatics Consultation

Bioinformatics study planning, including sample and metadata structure, replicates, comparison planning and downstream analysis strategy.

Omics & NGS Data Analysis

Quality control, exploratory analysis, RNA-seq and differential expression, functional analysis, single-cell analysis, metagenomics, metabarcoding, ATAC-seq, methylation analysis, resistome analysis, PhIP-seq, small RNA-seq and preparation of figures, tables and reports.

Pipelines, Automation & Troubleshooting

Python, R, and Bash workflows, HPC, SLURM/Torque support and data organization.

Training for Biologists & Research Teams

Python, R, and Bash for biologists, basic bioinformatics, sequencing basics, biological databases: SRA, GEO, UCSC, etc., biological data file formats, research data organization, HPC and Alliance cluster basics and practical project-based training.

Mentorship & Scientific Guidance

Scientific and technical guidance for students, trainees and research teams, including project planning, code review, data interpretation and workflow documentation.

Research Data Management & Governance

Support for organizing research data, metadata, documentation, access-controlled files, reproducible project structure, and responsible handling of sensitive biological and genomic datasets.

Training

Capacity building for biologists and research teams

Training can be offered as one-on-one sessions, group workshops, or project-based mentoring using examples that match your team’s data and goals.

  • Python, R, and Bash for biologists
  • Basic bioinformatics concepts
  • Sequencing technologies and NGS basics
  • FASTA, FASTQ, BAM, VCF, and GTF/GFF files
  • NCBI, SRA, GEO, GO, BLAST, and public databases
  • Linux and Bash for researchers
  • HPC and Alliance cluster basics
  • Research data organization and reproducibility
  • Workflow documentation and handover
  • Practical project-based training
